Why do we need to take good sleep?

  • Sleep is an essential function that allows our body and mind to recharge, leaving you fresh and alert when you wake up.
  • A good sleep at night is as important as regular exercise and a balanced diet is.
  • Poor and irregular sleeping patterns can adversely affect your productivity and it can also lead to hormonal imbalances.
  • Improper or less sleep causes fatigue which leads to stress and it can affect mental health of a person to a great extent.
  • Adequate amount of sleep also improves memory and increases concentration power.
