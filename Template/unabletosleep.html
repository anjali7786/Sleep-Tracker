Food and Habits

• Don't drink any caffeinated beverages (like soda or iced tea) after about 3:00 in the afternoon.
 • Green, leafy vegetables, pumpkin seeds, bananas, avocados, beans, and nuts and seeds are all foods rich in magnesium, which helps muscles relax and induces a sleepy state.
  • Moderate alcohol consumption since it can throw off your sleep cycles even if it makes you sleepy at first.
  • Try not to have your dinner too late so that you aren’t still digesting at bedtime and are at less risk of acid reflux. Be extra careful with spicy and oily foods late in the evening.
